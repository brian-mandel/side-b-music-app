# Side B

A proof-of-concept music rating and discovery app — think Letterboxd for music. 
Built in a weekend as my first experiment with AI-assisted development using Lovable.

**[Live Demo](https://side-b.lovable.app)**

## Screenshots

**Home Feed**
Discovery layer combining trending albums, structured ratings, and social engagement in a single surface.

**Album Detail**
Aggregate ratings, discussion, and take submission.

**User Profile**
Top albums, follower graph, and recent takes.

**Social Layer**
Follow users and engage with their takes.

## What It Does

- Structured album rating system
- Social feed of user takes
- User profiles with Top 3 albums
- Trending and new release discovery
- Follow other users

## Why I Built It

Music discovery platforms tend to optimize for volume and virality over quality 
of engagement. I wanted to explore what a music community might look like if it 
rewarded structured, thoughtful interaction instead of low-effort hot takes.

Side B is an experiment in product mechanics: how ratings, feeds, and discovery 
systems can be designed to encourage signal over noise, and a foundation for 
eventually layering AI recommendations on top of explicit user preferences rather 
than opaque algorithmic guesses.
